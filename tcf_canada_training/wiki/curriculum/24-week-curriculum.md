# 24-Week Curriculum

## Track Selection

| Baseline | Start week | Calendar length | Weekly load |
|---|---:|---:|---:|
| A2 or below | 1 | 24 weeks | 18-22 hours |
| B1 range | 5 | 20 weeks | 16-18 hours |
| Low B2 / unstable NCLC 7 | 9 | 16 weeks | 14-16 hours |
| Stable B2 or low C1 | 13 | 12 weeks | 12-15 hours |

The first action is a dual diagnostic: Ev@lang plus a TCF-style baseline.

## Weekly Blocks

Weeks 1-4: core systems.

Weeks 5-8: B1 transaction and narration.

Weeks 9-12: bridge to B2.

Weeks 13-16: B2 consolidation.

Weeks 17-20: C1 safety buffer.

Weeks 21-24: full mocks and taper.

## Daily Spine

| Day | Standard plan |
|---|---|
| Monday | Listening, grammar, vocabulary/SRS, pronunciation |
| Tuesday | Reading, grammar/vocabulary consolidation, writing Task 1 or 2 |
| Wednesday | Listening, solo speaking, live speaking, error log |
| Thursday | Reading, writing Task 2 or 3, reformulation |
| Friday | Listening, speaking Task 2 or 3, grammar repair, pronunciation |
| Saturday | Mini-mock or full timed block |
| Sunday | Dashboard review, flashcards, light immersion, rest |
