# Prompt Bank

This note tracks original practice prompts. The app contains the first seed set; future additions should be added here first or mirrored here after app updates.

## Speaking Task 2 Scenarios

- Rent an apartment and compare furnished versus unfurnished options.
- Ask a daycare about availability, hours, fees, and registration documents.
- Open a bank account and clarify fees, transfers, and card limits.
- Dispute an incorrect phone or internet bill.
- Schedule a medical appointment and describe symptoms politely.
- Ask about a professional training course and compare schedules.
- Clarify transport options after a cancelled train.
- Report lost baggage and ask about compensation.
- Ask about work schedules and remote-work rules.
- Compare two housing options before moving.

## Speaking Task 3 Topics

- Remote work improves work-life balance.
- Public transport should be cheaper than driving.
- Schools should teach nutrition and digital literacy.
- Social media helps people stay informed.
- Cities should limit cars downtown.
- Volunteering should be encouraged among newcomers.
- Bilingualism is a professional advantage.
- Employers should offer flexible schedules.
