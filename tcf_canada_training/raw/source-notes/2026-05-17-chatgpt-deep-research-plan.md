## Extracted Design Inputs

- TCF Canada has four mandatory components: listening, reading, writing, and speaking.
- Training should target reliable NCLC 7 in all four abilities and build toward an NCLC 9-style buffer.
- Use a dual diagnostic: Ev@lang plus a TCF-style baseline.
- Choose a 12, 16, 20, or 24 week track depending on baseline.
- Train all four skills every week.
- Use one-pass listening habits and strict writing word-count discipline.
- Use FEI/official resources first, then supplementary manuals and skill-building resources.
- Treat mock comprehension scaling as an estimate because FEI does not publish a simple raw-score conversion.
- Use a 20-point production rubric for speaking and writing training.
- Use error logs to assign remediation by failure mode.
- Postpone booking if any skill remains below target near the booking decision point.

## Implementation Interpretation

The system should become a daily practice cockpit plus an Obsidian memory layer, not merely a static study plan.
