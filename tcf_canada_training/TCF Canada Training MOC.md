## Current Operating Rule

The training system should help the learner practice every week across listening, reading, writing, and speaking. It should optimize for reliably clearing NCLC 7 in all four abilities and building toward an NCLC 9-style buffer where possible.

## Non-Negotiables

- Use official FEI and IRCC information as the source of truth for format, timing, retake windows, validity, and immigration thresholds.
- Do not claim that a mock raw score equals an official FEI score.
- Mark comprehension scores as readiness estimates because FEI's QCM scaling is not public.
- Track all four skills every week.
- Maintain word-count discipline for writing.
- Enforce one-pass listening behavior in practice.
- Keep stable decisions in Obsidian, not only in chat or app state.
